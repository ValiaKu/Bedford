Building Overview Summary Specification Accommodation

The Avenue offers 71,300 sq ft of high specification office space.

Designed by Sterling Prize nominated architects, Bennetts Associates, The Avenue is set to become the landmark building for the area. Contemporary artist Lilah Fowler has been commissioned to design a piece of public artwork to accentuate The Avenue’s façade.

Each floor plate has been designed to maximise flexibility of use to accommodate all working environments, be it an open space, informal creative set-up or a more structured offering.

Light is abundant throughout, and stylish features incorporated, from the contemporary bathrooms through to the roof gardens on the upper levels, which provide stunning views over the capital, overlooking the iconic British Museum and beyond.
