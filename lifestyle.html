Overview Local Amenities Leisure

The Avenue is situated amongst a diverse mix of world-class amenities and lifestyle.

Charlotte Street and its surrounds are renowned for culinary excellence, with the first outpost of Japanese gem, Roka, and the stylish Firmdale boutique offering, the Charlotte Street Hotel, all in close proximity. Store Street has established itself as an enclave for the discerning crowd, with several contemporary art galleries, independent boutique bike shop Cloud 9 Cycles, and Store Street Espresso, an ideal spot for lunch or to relax with a flat white.
