Offering 13,970 sq ft of retail space, The Avenue is at the forefront of the Tottenham Court Road transformation.

The Avenue, 251 Tottenham Court Road is situated at the intersection between Soho, Fitzrovia and Bloomsbury. The area is uniquely positioned to benefit from the Elizabeth line infrastructure project and will continue to prosper as a seven day a week thriving office, residential and retail district.

Situated in a prominent position, 100 metres from Oxford Street the scheme includes up to 5 units totalling 13,970 sq ft over ground and lower ground.

You’re in good company at The Avenue with a rapidly developing local area and exciting new local retailers.

Global media enterprises and corporate giants are finding a new home in the West End, attracted by the exceptional transport and amenities it offers.

Designed by Sterling Prize nominated architects, Bennetts Associates, The Avenue is set to become the landmark building for the area. Contemporary artist Lilah Fowler has been commissioned to design a piece of public artwork to accentuate The Avenue’s façade.
